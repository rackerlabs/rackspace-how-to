To ensure that the SMTP settings for your iPhone, iPad, or iPod are correctly configured, perform the following steps:

1. From the home screen on your device, tap **Settings** and then tap **Mail, Contacts, Calendars**.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/1_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" /> <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/2_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

2. Under **Accounts**, tap the email account.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/3_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

3. Tap **Account**.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/4_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

4. Scroll down and tap **SMTP** under **Outgoing Mail Server**.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/5_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

5. Tap your primary server (**secure.emailsrvr.com**).

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/7_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

6. Tap **Authentication**.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/8-revised.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

7. Ensure that **Password** is selected rather than **MD5 Challenge-Response**.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/9_0.jpg" width="320" height="480" /> <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/010.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

8. Tap the button on the top-left to go to the previous screen, and then tap **Done**.

    <img src="https://8026b2e3760e2433679c-fffceaebb8c6ee053c935e8915a3fbe7.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/field/image/011.jpg" width="320" height="480" />

To ensure that the SMTP settings for your iPhone, iPad, or iPod are correctly configured, perform the following steps:

1. From the home screen on your device, tap **Settings** and then tap **Mail, Contacts, Calendars**.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/1_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" /> <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/2_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

2. Under **Accounts**, tap the email account.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/3_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

3. Tap **Account**.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/4_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

4. Scroll down and tap **SMTP** under **Outgoing Mail Server**.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/5_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

5. Tap your primary server (**secure.emailsrvr.com**).

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/7_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

6. Tap **Authentication**.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/8-revised.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

7. Ensure that **Password** is selected rather than **MD5 Challenge-Response**.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/9_0.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" /> <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/010.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />

8. Tap the button on the top-left to go to the previous screen, and then tap **Done**.

    <img src="{% asset_path rackspace-email/updating-password-authentication-method-on-your-ios-device/011.jpg %}" width="320" height="480" />
